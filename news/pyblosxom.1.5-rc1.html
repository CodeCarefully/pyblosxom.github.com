Fri, 29 Jan 2010

PyBlosxom 1.5-rc1 released

It's been two years since the last release. Since then the world has turned, tools have improved (thank you Sphinx!), and PyBlosxom has undergone a lot of changes. The list of changes is really long. As such, this is a release candidate to get some more testing.

Download here:

https://sourceforge.net/projects/pyblosxom/files/

If you have any problems, please toss a bug in the bug tracker, say hi on IRC (#pyblosxom on irc.freenode.net), or send an email to the pyblosxom-users mailing list.

/will (willhelm at users dot sourceforge dot net)
