Submitting items to the registry

SourceForge has our file system as read-only which prevents us from handling registry submissions automatically. Sorry about that.

Submitting a new plugin

Submit a plugin to the registry by emailing willg at bluesock dot org the following information:

  • your name as it should appear on the site
  • name of your plugin
  • category you think it should go in
  • a description of your plugin and any notes users should know about
  • the url for the page about your plugin
  • the url to download your plugin

Allow me a few days to turn around the request--usually I (Will) am pretty quick about it unless my car has a flat tire or I'm somewhere far away. If I have any problems, I'll email you back.

Submitting a new flavour

Submit a new flavour to the flavour registry by emailing willg at bluesock dot org the following information:

  • your name as it should appear on the site
  • a description of the flavour including any special instructions
  • the url of a .png screenshot of your flavour in action
  • the url of the .tar.gz file

Allow me a few days to turn around the request--usually I (Will) am pretty quick about it unless my car has a flat tire or I'm somewhere far away. If I have any problems, I'll email you back.

Updating a plugin

If you want to update the details for a plugin or want to comment on a plugin, send an email to willg at bluesock dot org with the details.

Allow me a few days to turn around the request--usually I (Will) am pretty quick about it unless my car has a flat tire or I'm somewhere far away.
