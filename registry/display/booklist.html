book list

name: book list
author: Will Kahn-Greene
home page: http://bluesock.org/~willg/cgi-bin/devtrac.cgi/wiki/booklist
download: http://bluesock.org/~willg/cgi-bin/devtrac.cgi/wiki/booklist
license:
category: display
tags:
last edited: Jul 10, 2012 (Tuesday)

Reads from a :: delimited file a series of books and how far you are through reading them. It displays the 5 least done books in the $(booklist) variable. If you go to blog/booklist, it shows the complete list of books.
